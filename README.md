# SEO GEO Audit Skill

One unified skill for technical SEO, on-page optimization, EEAT review, GEO readiness, entity clarity, and authority assessment.

This skill is built for teams that do not want fragmented audits. Instead of separating technical diagnostics, content review, trust evaluation, and AI visibility analysis into isolated workflows, it produces one structured report and one prioritized roadmap.

## What It Does

- Audits technical SEO health
- Reviews on-page SEO quality
- Evaluates content clarity and trust signals
- Assesses GEO and AI citation readiness
- Surfaces entity and brand signal gaps
- Packages findings into executive, operator, or specialist outputs

## Why This Skill Is Different

Most audits answer only one of these questions:

- Is the site technically healthy?
- Is the page well-optimized?
- Is the brand trustworthy?
- Is the content ready for AI-driven discovery?

This skill answers all of them in one workflow.

It is designed for:

- founders and executives who want a clear growth diagnosis
- SEO operators who need a practical action plan
- consultants who want one consistent audit structure
- teams optimizing for both search rankings and AI visibility

## Audit Layers

### 1. Technical SEO

- crawlability and indexability
- performance and rendering
- redirects and canonical signals
- security headers and HTTPS posture
- schema and metadata hygiene
- mobile and accessibility risks

### 2. On-Page SEO

- titles and meta descriptions
- heading structure
- search intent alignment
- internal linking
- media and image handling
- content depth and structure

### 3. GEO and AI Visibility

- answer-first formatting
- semantic extractability
- quotable statements and evidence structure
- AI crawler friendliness
- machine-readable content and identity signals

### 4. Entity, Trust, and Authority

- author and editorial transparency
- organization identity consistency
- about, contact, and policy coverage
- third-party credibility signals
- brand disambiguation and authority indicators

## Output Modes

### Boss Mode

Short, decision-ready summary with:

- current state
- key business risks
- top opportunities
- priority actions

### Operator Mode

Execution-focused report with:

- observed issues
- strategic assessments
- P0 / P1 / P2 roadmap
- validation gaps

### Specialist Mode

Full diagnostic report with:

- layered findings
- scoring logic
- assumptions
- data limitations
